# Desktop

- "Big Picture"/10-foot display mode
  
  *For use in a home-theater or hi-fi setup; full-screen interface with large text and simple UI elements to scroll with a remote or gamepad. Dynamic visuals while playing music to prevent screen burn.*

- Keyboard Media Control support

- Cross-Device Sync

  *Listening data sync, potential Wi-Fi LAN/VPN based music data transfer between PCs and mobile*

- Discord Rich Presence

  *"Listening To: (blank)" in your Discord status*

- More streaming data import
  - YouTube Music
  - Spotify
  - Apple Music - Sign-In as XML alternative

- Automatic music tag and album art search

  *Further automated cleanup of bad tag data*

- More "smart playlists"
  - Top listened albums
  - Recommended downloads (top songs with no local file)
  - On Repeat (most listened lately)
  - Forgotten Favorites (lots of plays but not played recently)

- Right click track options:
  - "Go To Artist" and "Go To Album" options
  - "Play Next" or "Add to Queue" function
 
- Queue Functionality
  - "Start Queue" or "Add To Queue" by right-clicking tracks
  - Sidebar pane - quickly open and close to see queue, drag tracks into it, and even save it as a playlist
  - When queue started, if already in some previous play queue (like an album, playlist, library, etc.) save point and return to it after queue finishes

- App, period

  *Android first, iOS maybe later*

- Wi-Fi File Sync

  *Automatically send music to your phone over a LAN by playlist, album, file, or your whole library*

- Listening Data Sync

  *Update play counts, recently played, etc. automatically across all your devices*

- Landscape "Now Playing" screen

  *Show what you're listening to in style when your phone's in a stand on your desk or in your car*
